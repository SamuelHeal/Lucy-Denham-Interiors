Services

Interior Design & Decorating

My passion is helping my clients turn their house into a totally unique sanctuary away from the stresses of everyday life—a space that completes the way they want to live. Whether it be one room or an entire property, I can bring your dream to life.

Styling for Sale

First impressions count, and nothing could be truer when it comes to presenting your property for sale. Your home will be one of many that potential buyers will view, so it is vital it is presented to make a lasting impression. My Styling for Sale program will speed up the sale process and maximize the sale price.

Home Decluttering

When the time comes to downsize, many home owners have trouble downsizing their possessions. Many feel overwhelmed, anxious, and defeated around the idea of decluttering their homes. I specialize in home decluttering, and can guide you in organising your life’s possessions and readying your home for the next owners to enjoy.
